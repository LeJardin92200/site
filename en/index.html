Welcome to Le Jardin

From Monday to Friday, starting at 7:00 AM, David welcomes you to Le Jardin — a brasserie full of heart and flavor, nestled in the center of Neuilly-sur-Seine.

Just steps from the Louis Vuitton Foundation and Jardin d'Acclimatation, Le Jardin is more than just a brasserie — it's a cozy corner where people come to eat well, drink well, and feel right at home.

A team that loves doing things right (especially in the kitchen):

  • David – Passionate chef and manager 🍲
  • Our kitchen assistant – Quiet but deadly with a knife 🔪
  • Our waitress – Guaranteed smile from 8 a.m. ☕
  • Our bartender – Cocktail wizard 🎩🍸

🍷 Quick lunch, happy hour or a coffee on the terrace? Come say hello!
